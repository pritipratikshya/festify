About Festify 2025

Festify is BJBians annual student festival — a four-day celebration of technology, creativity, and culture. It brings together the brightest minds from campuses across India to compete, collaborate, and create.

Whether you're a coder, designer, speaker, musician, or just someone who loves to explore new ideas — Festify has something for you. From hackathons and workshops to cultural nights and networking sessions, every moment is crafted to inspire.

This year's theme: "Build the World You Imagine."
